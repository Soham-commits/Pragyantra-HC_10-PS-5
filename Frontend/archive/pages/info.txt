FEATURES SECTION:

Feature 1 – Symptom-Based AI Assistant:
Users can describe their symptoms through a conversational AI assistant that asks relevant follow-up questions and provides preliminary health insights based on trusted medical knowledge.

Feature 2 – Medical Image Screening:
MediQ supports AI-assisted screening of medical images such as chest X-rays and skin lesion scans to identify potential abnormalities at an early stage.

Feature 3 – Explainable AI (Grad-CAM):
For abnormal scan results, MediQ provides visual heatmaps that highlight regions influencing the AI’s decision, improving transparency and user trust.

Feature 4 – Unified Health ID:
Each user is assigned a unique Health ID that securely stores and links their medical history, reports, and scan results across visits and doctors.

Feature 5 – Automated Medical Reports:
The platform generates structured, easy-to-understand medical reports that can be shared directly with doctors or healthcare providers.

Feature 6 – Doctor & Hospital Recommendations:
Based on symptoms and screening results, MediQ suggests nearby hospitals, clinics, and doctors for further consultation.

DOCTOR PORTAL SECTION:

Doctor Portal Description:
MediQ provides a dedicated doctor portal that allows healthcare professionals to securely access patient reports and medical history (with consent), add diagnoses, prescriptions, and follow-up notes, and streamline patient care.

Doctor Portal Capabilities:
• View patient history via Health ID
• Access AI-generated reports and scans
• Add clinical notes and prescriptions
• Support continuity of care


AI STANDARDS & SAFETY SECTION:

AI Safety Statement:
MediQ uses AI strictly for preliminary health screening and decision support. All AI outputs are explainable, transparent, and designed to assist—not replace—qualified medical professionals.

Privacy & Security:
• Secure authentication and role-based access
• Patient-controlled data sharing
• Encrypted storage of medical data
• No unauthorized access to health records
